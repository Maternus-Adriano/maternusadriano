Background & Mission

About Maternus

A healthcare systems professional whose work sits at the intersection of institutional governance, public–private partnership, and service delivery — in one of East Africa's most complex health environments.

Maternus Adriano brings over six years of progressive leadership across healthcare operations in Tanzania, working with dialysis networks, nutrition foundations, and hospital management within public–private partnership frameworks. Currently serving as Senior Business, Operations & Administration Manager at NSK Hospitals Ltd, an institution responsible in managing regional hospitals operating under a PPP model with the Ministry of Health Zanzibar. He also carries the added responsibility of Chief of Staff to the Director's Office.

His career spans roles at Africa Healthcare Network, where he was responsible as a Regional Operations Manager for a new Southern Highlands Zone — overseeing growth and expansion of dialysis care across all the regions of the Southern Highlands — and at ONA Foundation, where he currently serves as a member of the Board. His journey there began as Project Administrator, where he helped create nutrition programmes and contributed to the National School Feeding Guideline.

Maternus is grounded in the belief that meaningful improvement in public health outcomes requires systemic thinking — aligning institutions, people, and processes toward shared national goals. He is available for collaboration, advisory engagement, and public service contribution.
